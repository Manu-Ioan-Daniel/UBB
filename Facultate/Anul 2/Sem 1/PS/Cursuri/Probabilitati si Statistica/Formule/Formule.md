1. $A \cup \overline{A} = \Omega$  
2. $A \cap \overline{A} = \emptyset$  
3. $\overline{\overline{A}} = A$
4. $P(A \in M) := \frac{\text{masura}(M)}{\text{masura}(D)}$
5. $P\Big(\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty} A_n\Big) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} P(A_n)$
6. $P(\overline{A}) = 1 - P(A)$, cu $0 \le P(A) \le 1$  
7. $P(\emptyset) = 0$  
8. $P(A \setminus B) = P(A) - P(A \cap B)$  
9. $A \subseteq B \Rightarrow P(A) \le P(B)$  
10. $P(A \cup B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A \cap B)$  
11. $P(A\cap B)= P(A) \cdot P(B)$ (daca A si B sunt evenimente independente)
12. $P \big(\bigcap_{i=1}^{m} B_i\big)=\prod_{i=1}^{m} P(B_i)$  unde $m \in \{2,3, . . . , n\}$ si $B_1, B_2, . . . , B_n$ sunt evenimente n-independente
13. $P(A|B) = \frac {P(A \cap B)}{P(B)}$
14. $P(A \cap B) = P(B) \cdot P(A \mid B) = P(A) \cdot P(B \mid A)$,  
15. $P( \overline {A} \mid B) = 1 - P(A \mid B)$.
