Cunoscand distributiile variabilelor aleatoare independente (discrete) $X$ si $Y$, cum se determina distributia pentru $X + Y$, $X \cdot Y$?

$$
\begin {align*}
(X+Y) &\sim 
\begin {pmatrix}
k \\
p_k
\end {pmatrix}
,\quad k=a+b,\ a \in \{x_1,x_2 \ldots \},\ b \in \{y_1,y_2 \ldots\} \ unde \\ 
&p_k = P(X+Y=k) = \sum_{a+b=k} P(X=a,Y=b)
\end {align*}

$$
$$
\begin {align*}
(X \cdot Y) &\sim 
\begin {pmatrix}
k \\
p_k
\end {pmatrix}

&p_k = P(X \cdot Y=k) = \sum_{a \cdot b=k} P(X=a,Y=b)
